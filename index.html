Institutes Under Our Province

St. Mary’s School

St. Mary’s Academy/High School in Ponda, Goa, is a reputable co-educational institution providing education in Ponda, with a focus on academic excellence. It has a strong local reputation, rated highly in the area, offering a supportive environment for students' personal and educational growth

Presentation College

Presentation Convent High School/Higher Secondary is a Catholic minority institution, commonly run by the Franciscan Hospitaller Sisters or Presentation Sisters, focusing on holistic, value-based education for girls (and sometimes co-ed). Founded on principles of character development, these schools emphasize academic excellence, moral integrity, and social service, often spanning from primary to senior secondary levels.

Health Centre

A health centre is a community-based facility providing essential, accessible primary healthcare services, acting as the first point of contact for patients. These centres offer comprehensive services, including general medical care, preventive care, maternal/child health services, and, in some cases, mental health and dental services, often focusing on underserved areas.
